WNC Dam status post Hurricane Helene

About this site

In late September 2024, Hurricane Helene swept through western NC, leaving behind a wake of destruction, while also leaving people questioning if the states dam infrastructure would be able to withstand another natural disaster like this one.

Inspired by the article "Dams problematic in NC during Helene flooding, future role unclear", this map shows the potenial hazard status of dams in specfic North Carolina Counties affected by Hurricane Helene, whcih are outlined by the NOAA in this report. Clicking any of the points will showcase the name of the dam.
